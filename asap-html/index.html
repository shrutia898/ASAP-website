Who We Are?

We are the Association of South Asians at Purdue, an inclusive student student organization that celebrates and represents the diverse cultures, traditions, and perspectives of South Asian communities within the Purdue University campus.

What We Do?

This organization serves as a hub for individuals who have a connection to South Asia or a genuine interest in exploring its rich heritage.Through a variety of events, activities, and initiatives, ASAP fosters a sense of belonging and offers a platform for its members to learn, share, and grow.

Why We Do It?

The core mission of the "Association of South Asians at Purdue" revolves around two pivotal goals: celebrating the rich tapestry of South Asian culture and traditions while fostering a strong sense of community among like-minded individuals who share these values.

Since When?

This club started in the fall of 2022, when a group of passionate and dedicated students came together with a shared vision: to create a space on campus where South Asian culture, traditions, and values could be celebrated and shared with the wider university community.
